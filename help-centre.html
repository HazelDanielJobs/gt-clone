Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account with GTBank? + −

You can open an account by visiting any GTBank branch with a valid means of identification, a utility bill, and one passport-sized photo. You can also start the process online through our website or on the GTBank mobile app.

What documents do I need to open an account? + −

You will need a valid form of ID (International Passport, National ID Card, Driver's License, or Voter's Card), a utility bill for address verification, and a passport photograph.

How do I activate my GTBank debit card? + −

Your debit card is automatically activated after you set your 4-digit PIN at any GTBank ATM. Insert your card, select "PIN Change", enter the default PIN, and create a new PIN of your choice.

How do I reset my internet banking password? + −

Go to the internet banking login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your User ID and follow the on-screen instructions. You will receive a reset link via your registered email address.

What is the *737# USSD code for? + −

*737# is our USSD banking channel. You can use it to transfer funds, check your balance, buy airtime, pay bills, and access loans — all from any phone, without internet.

How do I report a lost or stolen card? + −

You can immediately block your card via the GTBank mobile app, internet banking, or by calling our 24/7 contact centre at 0700-GTCONNECT (08282066328). Visit any branch for a replacement.

What are the charges for international transfers? + −

International transfer charges vary based on the destination, amount, and type of transfer. Please visit any GTBank branch or contact our customer care for specific rates and charges.

How do I update my BVN on my account? + −

Visit any GTBank branch with your valid ID to link or update your BVN. You can also dial *737*0# to check if your BVN is already linked to your account.
